# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the BengoBox Food Delivery project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy, kindness, and respect toward other people
- Being welcoming and inclusive to people of all backgrounds
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

- The use of sexualised language or imagery; harassment of any kind
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards of acceptable behaviour. Appropriate and fair corrective action will be taken in response to any behaviour deemed inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project team at [security@bengobox.com](mailto:security@bengobox.com). All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.

## Enforcement Guidelines

1. **Correction** – Private communication clarifying expectations
2. **Warning** – Explicit warning with consequences for continued behaviour
3. **Temporary Ban** – Contributor temporarily banned from interaction
4. **Permanent Ban** – Contributor removed from the community
